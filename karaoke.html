  • Iya's Korean Kitchen is the brainchild of two dynamic and visionary entrepreneurs Indraja Khaladkar and Shrihari Mamadapur, whose journey began with a humble investment of Rs 500 and a shared passion of taking Korean cuisine to everyone. In September 2020, armed with determination and a dream, they embarked on their culinary adventure by starting a cloud kitchen. Fuelled by their unwavering commitment and relentless drive, they poured their hearts and souls into crafting authentic Korean dishes that captivated the taste buds of Pune's discerning food enthusiasts. Through sheer dedication and innovation, they transformed their modest beginnings into a culinary sensation, establishing their first restaurant in May 2022. Winning hearts & earning the title of Pune's favorite Korean restaurant.
